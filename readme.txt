My idea is to have it so you can buy multiple cars and upgrade each individually.  
One car you drive yourself and you can hire drivers for the other cars

The race you assign your car to you would have to start manually.  The races you
assign paid driver cars to would start automatically, but reward less because
you have to pay the driver

There will still be different types of upgrades for the cars, but instead of it
just increasing a single performance stat different attributes of the car will
be increased.  Races will give better rewards when you assign a car with
attributes that match those needed for that race
